Interests

Listening to Music!

I know this doesn't sound like much of an interest, but hear me out.
I'm a frequent member of online music communities like Rate Your Music, where I review newely released albums, or classics that I haven't gotten around to yet. Online forums are also where I find my next listen!
I also hold a weekly radio show on 91.5 FM, WMFO, Tufts Freeform Radio, with my good friend. We take deep dives into overlooked emotions, and play music that we think reflects the feeling associated with that emotion. Listen in to 'Affective Meltdown' every Wednesday at 6 AM!

Running!

A passion for running 'runs' in the family, as my father competed collegietely in track and field, and my mother is a multi-time marathoner, and still runs marathons to this day! Beyond this, both of my brohters ran in high-school. This influenced me to start running competetively before I reached middle school, and I haven't looked back since!
Currently, I compete for Tufts University's varsity cross country and track and field teams. My experiences competing for a collegiete team have provided me with memories that I will never forget, and teammates, that I will hold dear to me for the rest of my life.

The Outdoors!

I'm very fortunate that two interests of mine compliment eachother, as my love of running allows me to explore the great outdoors!
As I grow older, I find that my appreciation of everything outside my bedroom grows, and I get to share this sentiment with my friends through the trips we take together! This past summer we spent a week in Acadia National Park, and it was the trip of a lifetime! I'll never forget the view at the top of Cadalliac Mountain.
I also enjoy visiting my cousins in the Coloradian Rockies, and getting to explore all that the low-elevation dwellers (like myself) am barred from!
